5. Incident Map & Logs
Data

The Incident Map and Incident Logs aggregate data from multiple external sources:

  • NSW Rural Fire Service (RFS) – Bush fire incidents and Fire Danger Ratings
  • Bureau of Meteorology (BOM) – Weather warnings and radar
  • Live Traffic NSW – Traffic incidents, roadwork, floods, fires, major events
  • Waze – Crowdsourced road hazards, police reports, roadwork
  • Endeavour Energy / Ausgrid – Power outages (planned and unplanned)
  • Beachwatch / BeachSafe NSW – Beach conditions and safety
  • Traffic cameras – NSW traffic camera feeds
  • Aviation cameras – Airport webcam feeds
  • Pager messages – Aggregated from pager.forcequit.xyz

Data retention: Historical incident data is retained for up to 7 days and then automatically deleted.

User-submitted incidents (via Map Editor) are automatically removed after their configured expiry time (1–48 hours).

6. Discord Bot
Bot

The NSW PSN Discord Bot provides real-time alert notifications to Discord servers.

By adding the bot to your server, you agree that:

  • Server administrators control which alerts are enabled and where they are posted
  • The bot stores server ID, channel ID, and alert configuration preferences
  • Pager alert subscriptions may include specific capcodes (agency codes)
  • Alert messages link incidents for context (e.g., showing related pager messages)
  • The bot may be removed from servers or discontinued at any time

Bot administrators must not:

  • Configure alerts in channels accessible to the general public for operational purposes
  • Use alerts to coordinate emergency response or self-deployment
