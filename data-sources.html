📊 Data Sources & Suppliers

All external data sources and APIs used to power the live map and incident feeds.

ℹ️ About Our Data
Info

This site aggregates publicly available data from various NSW government agencies and third-party providers. All data is fetched in near real-time and cached briefly to improve performance. We do not modify or alter the source data beyond formatting for display.

Forcequit is not affiliated with any of these data providers. Data accuracy and availability depends on the source systems.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This data is provided for informational purposes only. Always verify critical information directly with official sources before making decisions. Data may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate. Do not rely solely on this site for emergency situations.

⚡ Ausgrid
Power

Power outage statistics and summary data from the Ausgrid network covering Sydney, Central Coast and Hunter regions.

Data: Outage counts (planned/unplanned), affected customers

Source: ausgrid.com.au

⚡ Endeavour Energy
Power

Current power outage locations across the Endeavour Energy network covering Greater Western Sydney, Blue Mountains, Southern Highlands, Illawarra and South Coast.

Data: Outage locations, affected customers, estimated restoration times, crew status

Source: endeavourenergy.com.au

⚡ Essential Energy
Power

Power outage data from the Essential Energy network covering regional and rural NSW — including the North Coast, New England, Central West, Riverina and Far West regions.

Data: Outage locations, affected customers, cause/reason, estimated restoration, outage boundaries (KML polygons)

🚗 Live Traffic NSW
Traffic

Traffic incidents, roadwork, flood reports, fire hazards, major events and 800+ traffic camera feeds from Transport for NSW.

Data: Incidents, roadwork, floods, fire closures, major events, camera images

✈️ Airservices Australia
Aviation

Airport weather cameras from 50+ airports across Australia including Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and regional airports. Each location provides up to 4 camera angles (N/E/S/W).

Data: Live weather camera images, compass direction views, timestamps

Source: weathercams.airservicesaustralia.com

🔥 Central Watch (WatchTowers)
Live

Live fire watch tower camera feeds from 16 sites across NSW and the ACT, providing 27 camera angles for early bushfire detection. Images refresh every 30 seconds.

Data: Live camera images (Guard & Sentry), tower locations, elevation data

Source: centralwatch.watchtowers.io

🚨 Waze

Community-reported traffic data including hazards, police sightings, accidents, traffic jams, and road closures across NSW.

Data: Traffic hazards, police alerts, accidents, potholes, road closures, traffic jams, roadwork

Source: waze.com

🌧️ RainViewer

Weather radar overlay showing precipitation across NSW with past frames and short-term nowcast.

///what3words
Maps

Location encoding system that divides the world into 3m × 3m squares, each identified by a unique three-word address. Used on the incident map for precise location sharing and coordinate lookup.

Data: Three-word addresses, coordinate conversion (words → GPS & GPS → words)

Source: what3words.com

Fire watch tower camera platform providing early bushfire detection across NSW and the ACT. Powers the Central Watch camera layer on the incident map.

Data: Tower locations, camera positions, site elevation data

Source: watchtowers.io

📝 User Incidents
Community

Community-submitted incident reports via the Map Editor. Allows authenticated users to report incidents not captured by official data sources.

Data: User-reported incidents, locations, descriptions, images

Storage: Supabase (auto-removed after 1–48 hours)

📡 Update Frequency
Technical

Data is refreshed at different intervals depending on the source and page activity:

💡 Adaptive Collection: Data is collected every 1 minute when a data page is active, or every 2 minutes when idle (no page open).

🤖 Discord Bot: Alerts are polled every 60 seconds. Pager messages are polled every 30 seconds.

🔴 Real-time (~30-60 seconds)

  • Radio Feed (Active Units & Talkgroups)
  • Traffic Incidents, Floods, Fire Hazards
  • Traffic Cameras & Airport Weather Cameras
  • Central Watch Fire Tower Cameras (27 cameras)
  • RFS Major Incidents
  • Waze Hazards, Police & Traffic Jams

🟡 Frequent (~2-5 minutes)

  • Ausgrid & Endeavour Energy Outages
  • Roadwork, Major Events
  • BOM Weather Warnings
  • RFS Fire Danger Ratings

🟢 Periodic (~5-10 minutes)

  • News RSS Feeds (ABC, 9News, SMH, etc.)
  • Weather Conditions (Open-Meteo)
  • Weather Radar (RainViewer)
  • Beachwatch Water Quality
  • BeachSafe Patrol Status (SLSA)
