Data Sources & Suppliers

All external data sources and APIs used to power the live map and incident feeds.

About Our Data
Info

This site aggregates publicly available data from various NSW government agencies and third-party providers. All data is fetched in near real-time and cached briefly to improve performance. We do not modify or alter the source data beyond formatting for display.

Forcequit is not affiliated with any of these data providers. Data accuracy and availability depends on the source systems.

Disclaimer: This data is provided for informational purposes only. Always verify critical information directly with official sources before making decisions. Data may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate. Do not rely solely on this site for emergency situations.

NASA FIRMS
Satellite

Satellite-detected active fire hotspots across NSW and a small cross-border buffer, drawn as true pixel footprints. Sourced from the VIIRS sensor flying on three platforms — Suomi-NPP, NOAA-20 and NOAA-21 — for roughly 12-hourly orbital coverage. All near-real-time (typically ~3 hours after the satellite pass) at 375m resolution.

Data: Fire hotspot locations, brightness, fire radiative power (FRP), confidence, satellite/acquisition time

Technical

How often each source refreshes

Update Frequency
Technical

Data is refreshed at different intervals depending on the source and page activity:

Adaptive Collection: Data is collected every 1 minute when a data page is active, or every 2 minutes when idle (no page open).

Discord Bot: Alerts are polled every 60 seconds. Pager messages are polled every 30 seconds.

Real-time (~30-60 seconds)

  • Radio Feed (Active Units & Talkgroups)
  • Traffic Incidents, Floods, Fire Hazards
  • Traffic Cameras & Airport Weather Cameras
  • Central Watch Fire Tower Cameras (27 cameras)
  • RFS Major Incidents
  • Waze Hazards, Police & Traffic Jams
  • MarineTraffic Vessel Positions (AIS)

Frequent (~2-5 minutes)

  • Ausgrid & Endeavour Energy Outages
  • Roadwork, Major Events
  • BOM Weather Warnings
  • RFS Fire Danger Ratings
  • NASA FIRMS Fire Hotspots

Periodic (~5-10 minutes)

  • News RSS Feeds (ABC, 9News, SMH, etc.)
  • Weather Conditions (Open-Meteo)
  • Weather Radar (RainViewer)
  • Beachwatch Water Quality
  • BeachSafe Patrol Status (SLSA)
