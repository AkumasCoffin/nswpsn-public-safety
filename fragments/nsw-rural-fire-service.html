🔥 NSW Rural Fire Service – Overview
NSW RFS
NSW Rural Fire Service Volunteer Fire Service

The NSW Rural Fire Service (NSW RFS) is the world’s largest volunteer fire service and the lead combat agency for bush and grass fires in New South Wales. It protects communities, property and the environment across around 95% of the state.
